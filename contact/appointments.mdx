## What to Bring to Your Appointment

### For All Patients

**Mandatory Documents:**
- Valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, or Passport)
- Previous medical records and prescriptions (if any)
- Insurance card or details (if applicable)
- Referral letter from another doctor (if referred)

**Medical Information:**
- List of current medications with dosages
- Known allergies to medications or substances
- Recent test reports or imaging (X-rays, ultrasounds, blood tests)
- Vaccination records (for pediatric patients)

<Info>
**First-Time Patients:**  
Arrive 15 minutes early to complete registration forms. Bring your ID proof and insurance details for a smooth registration process.

**Follow-Up Patients:**  
Bring your previous prescription and any completed tests. Your medical records are in our system, but physical copies help doctors review your progress.
</Info>

### For Specific Departments

**Cardiology:**
- Previous ECG reports
- List of cardiac symptoms with dates
- Blood pressure monitoring logs (if maintaining)
- Previous cardiac imaging (echo, angiography reports)

**Diabetes Clinic:**
- Blood sugar monitoring diary
- Current insulin/medication details
- Recent HbA1c reports
- Diet chart (if following any)

**Orthopedics:**
- Previous X-rays, MRI, or CT scans
- Details of injury or pain onset
- Any braces or supports currently used

**Obstetrics:**
- LMP (Last Menstrual Period) date
- Previous pregnancy reports
- Ultrasound scans
- Blood group report

**Surgery Consultation:**
- All diagnostic reports related to condition
- Previous surgery records
- Anesthesia history (if any complications)
- Current medication list

<Note>
If you're undergoing diagnostic tests before your appointment, bring those reports even if they're from outside facilities. This helps your doctor make informed decisions without repeating tests.
</Note>

## Appointment Policies

### Cancellation & Rescheduling

**Online Appointments:**
- Cancel or reschedule up to 4 hours before appointment time
- Log in to your account on website or mobile app
- Select "Manage Appointments" and follow prompts
- Full refund processed within 5-7 business days (if payment made)

**Phone Appointments:**
- Call the appointment desk at least 4 hours in advance
- Cancellation reference number will be provided
- Rescheduling offered based on doctor availability

**Late Cancellation Fee:**
- Cancellations within 4 hours of appointment: ₹200 fee
- No-shows without cancellation: ₹500 fee
- Fee waived for emergencies with valid proof

<Note>
If you're running late, call the department directly. We can often adjust your slot if notified within 15 minutes of appointment time.
</Note>

### Wait Time Policy

We value your time and strive to minimize waiting:

- **On-Time Appointments:** 90% of patients seen within 15 minutes of scheduled time
- **Delays:** If doctor is running more than 30 minutes late, we'll notify you via SMS
- **Options:** You can choose to wait, reschedule, or see another available doctor

**Track Your Wait:**
- Check real-time queue status on hospital TVs
- Receive SMS when your turn approaches
- Download our mobile app for live updates

### Emergency Override

Scheduled appointments may occasionally be delayed due to:
- Emergency cases requiring immediate attention
- Complicated cases taking longer than expected
- Unforeseen circumstances

We appreciate your understanding and patience in such situations.
