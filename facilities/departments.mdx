## Emergency Department

Our Emergency Department provides immediate medical attention for acute illnesses and injuries, operating 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

### Key Features

<Info>
  **Average Wait Time**: Under 15 minutes for critical cases, with triage system ensuring priority treatment based on severity
</Info>

- **Trauma Center**: Level II trauma center with dedicated resuscitation bays
- **Fast Track Unit**: Separate area for minor injuries and illnesses
- **Pediatric Emergency**: Child-friendly emergency area with specialized pediatric equipment
- **Observation Unit**: Short-stay unit for patients requiring extended monitoring
- **Ambulance Services**: Fleet of advanced life support ambulances with paramedic teams

### Staff & Expertise

- Emergency medicine physicians available 24/7
- Specialized trauma surgeons on-call
- Experienced emergency nurses with ACLS and PALS certification
- Radiology and laboratory services available round-the-clock
- Direct access to all specialist consultants

### Emergency Conditions Treated

- Cardiac emergencies (heart attacks, arrhythmias)
- Stroke and neurological emergencies
- Respiratory distress and asthma attacks
- Traumatic injuries (fractures, burns, wounds)
- Poisoning and overdoses
- Severe infections and sepsis
- Acute abdominal conditions

## Intensive Care Unit

Our ICU provides critical care for patients requiring constant monitoring and advanced life support, with a patient-to-nurse ratio ensuring personalized attention.

### ICU Capabilities

<Info>
  **Bed Capacity**: 24 ICU beds with individual monitoring stations and isolation capabilities for infection control
</Info>

- **Medical ICU**: For critically ill patients with complex medical conditions
- **Surgical ICU**: Post-operative care for major surgical procedures
- **Cardiac ICU**: Specialized care for heart attack and cardiac surgery patients
- **Neonatal ICU**: Level III NICU for premature and critically ill newborns

### Advanced Monitoring Systems

- Continuous cardiac monitoring with telemetry
- Invasive hemodynamic monitoring
- Multi-parameter vital signs monitoring
- Intracranial pressure monitoring
- Continuous EEG monitoring for neurological patients

### Life Support Equipment

- Mechanical ventilators with advanced modes
- Dialysis machines for renal support
- ECMO (Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation) capability
- Intra-aortic balloon pumps
- Defibrillators and external pacemakers

### ICU Team

- Intensivists (critical care specialists) available 24/7
- Dedicated ICU nursing staff with critical care training
- Respiratory therapists
- Clinical pharmacists
- Physiotherapists for early mobilization
- Nutritionists for specialized feeding protocols

## Surgical Department

Our Surgical Department offers comprehensive surgical services across multiple specialties, utilizing minimally invasive techniques whenever possible.

### Operating Theaters

<Info>
  **Theater Capacity**: 8 fully equipped modular operating rooms with laminar air flow and HEPA filtration systems
</Info>

- **General Surgery OT**: For abdominal and soft tissue procedures
- **Orthopedic OT**: With C-arm imaging and specialized orthopedic table
- **Neurosurgery OT**: Equipped with operating microscope and neuronavigation
- **Cardiac Surgery OT**: With cardiopulmonary bypass capability
- **Minimally Invasive Surgery Suite**: For laparoscopic and robotic procedures

### Surgical Specialties

#### General Surgery
- Abdominal surgery (appendectomy, hernia repair, gallbladder)
- Breast surgery and oncological procedures
- Thyroid and parathyroid surgery
- Colorectal surgery

#### Orthopedic Surgery
- Joint replacement (hip, knee, shoulder)
- Arthroscopic surgery
- Spine surgery
- Trauma and fracture fixation
- Sports medicine procedures

#### Neurosurgery
- Brain tumor surgery
- Spine surgery (discectomy, fusion)
- Vascular neurosurgery
- Minimally invasive neurosurgery

#### Cardiovascular Surgery
- Coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG)
- Valve repair and replacement
- Congenital heart defect correction
- Vascular surgery

#### Other Specialties
- ENT surgery (tonsillectomy, sinus surgery, cochlear implants)
- Ophthalmology (cataract, retinal surgery)
- Urology (kidney stones, prostate surgery)
- Plastic and reconstructive surgery

### Day Surgery Unit

- Dedicated facility for same-day surgical procedures
- Pre-operative assessment clinic
- Comfortable recovery areas
- Discharge within 6-8 hours for eligible procedures

## Maternity & Obstetrics

Our Maternity Department provides comprehensive care throughout pregnancy, delivery, and postpartum period in a comfortable, family-centered environment.

### Antenatal Services

<Info>
  **Delivery Statistics**: Over 2,500 deliveries annually with a 98% patient satisfaction rate
</Info>

- **Antenatal Clinic**: Regular checkups with obstetricians
- **High-Risk Pregnancy Management**: Maternal-fetal medicine specialists
- **Prenatal Screening**: Advanced ultrasound and genetic testing
- **Childbirth Education Classes**: Preparation for labor and parenting
- **Lactation Counseling**: Breastfeeding support and education

### Labor & Delivery

- **Labor Rooms**: 12 private labor rooms with ensuite facilities
- **Birthing Options**: Natural birth, water birth, and epidural anesthesia available
- **Delivery Suites**: 4 fully equipped delivery rooms
- **Operating Theater**: For cesarean sections with neonatal team present
- **Pain Management**: Epidural service available 24/7

### Postnatal Care

- **Private Rooms**: Comfortable rooms with rooming-in facilities
- **Mother-Baby Care**: Pediatricians examine all newborns
- **Breastfeeding Support**: Lactation consultants available daily
- **Postnatal Checkups**: Before discharge and follow-up appointments

### Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU)

- **Level III NICU**: 16-bed unit for premature and sick newborns
- **Specialized Equipment**: Incubators, phototherapy, ventilators
- **Neonatologists**: 24/7 availability
- **Kangaroo Mother Care**: Encouraged for stable premature babies
- **Parent Support**: Counseling and education for NICU parents
