### Building Systems

#### Power & Backup

<Info>
  **Uninterrupted Power**: Multiple redundancy layers ensure continuous operation even during extended outages
</Info>

- **Main Power**: High-capacity electrical infrastructure
- **Generator Backup**: 2 MW diesel generators with automatic switchover (within 10 seconds)

- **Potable Water**: Multi-stage filtration and UV sterilization
- **Hot Water**: Solar-assisted heating system with instant backup
- **Medical Gases**: Centralized piping for oxygen, medical air, nitrous oxide, vacuum
- **Emergency Water**: Underground storage tanks with 72-hour capacity
- **Wastewater Treatment**: On-site treatment plant for hospital effluent

## Hygiene & Infection Control

Maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and infection prevention is our top priority.

### Hygiene Protocols

<Info>
  **Infection Rates**: Hospital-acquired infection rates below 0.5%, significantly lower than national averages
</Info>

#### Hand Hygiene

- **Alcohol-Based Hand Rub**: Dispensers at every patient room entrance and throughout corridors
- **Handwashing Stations**: Medical-grade sinks in all clinical areas
- **WHO 5 Moments**: Strict adherence to hand hygiene guidelines
- **Monitoring**: Electronic monitoring system tracking compliance
- **Training**: Regular staff training and competency assessments

#### Environmental Cleaning

**Daily Cleaning Schedule**
- Patient rooms cleaned twice daily (morning and evening)
- Bathrooms sanitized after each use in high-traffic areas
- High-touch surfaces cleaned every 2 hours
- Operating theaters terminally cleaned after each procedure
- Public areas maintained continuously during operating hours

**Deep Cleaning Protocols**
- ICU rooms terminally cleaned between patients
- Operating theaters deep-cleaned weekly
- Entire floors deep-cleaned on rotating schedule
- UV-C disinfection for isolation rooms post-discharge

**Cleaning Products**
- Hospital-grade disinfectants effective against pathogens
- EPA-registered products with proven efficacy
- Color-coded cleaning equipment (prevents cross-contamination)
- Microfiber technology for superior cleaning

#### Sterilization Services

**Central Sterile Supply Department (CSSD)**

<Info>
  **Processing Capacity**: 3,000+ instrument trays processed daily with complete tracking and quality assurance
</Info>

- **Automated Systems**: High-capacity autoclaves and washer-disinfectors
- **Quality Control**: Biological indicators tested daily
- **Tracking System**: Barcode tracking of all sterile instruments
- **Standards Compliance**: ISO 13485 certified processes
- **Dedicated Zones**: Separate decontamination, assembly, and sterile storage areas

**Sterilization Methods**
- High-pressure steam sterilization (autoclaving)
- Ethylene oxide sterilization for heat-sensitive items
- Hydrogen peroxide plasma sterilization
- Chemical disinfection for endoscopes

### Infection Prevention Measures

#### Isolation Protocols

- **Contact Isolation**: For multidrug-resistant organisms
- **Droplet Isolation**: For respiratory infections
- **Airborne Isolation**: Negative pressure rooms for TB, measles, etc.
- **Protective Isolation**: Positive pressure rooms for immunocompromised patients
- **PPE Protocols**: Strict donning and doffing procedures

#### Surveillance Systems

- **Active Surveillance**: Infection control team monitors all inpatients
- **Microbiology Integration**: Real-time alerts for concerning pathogens
- **Outbreak Management**: Rapid response protocols
- **Antibiotic Stewardship**: Program to prevent antimicrobial resistance
- **Data Analytics**: Continuous monitoring of infection trends

#### Waste Management

**Segregation at Source**
- Color-coded bins for different waste categories
- Red bags for infectious waste
- Yellow bags for anatomical waste
- Blue bags for pharmaceutical waste
- Black bags for general waste

**Disposal Systems**
- On-site incinerator for biomedical waste
- Licensed contractors for hazardous waste
- Recycling program for paper and plastics
- Sharps containers in all clinical areas
- Trackable waste from generation to disposal

#### Air Quality Management

- **HEPA Filtration**: In operating theaters and critical care areas
- **Air Changes**: 15-25 air changes per hour in OTs and ICUs
- **Pressure Differentials**: Maintained between zones
- **Monitoring**: Continuous air quality monitoring
- **Maintenance**: Regular filter replacement and system validation

### Water Quality

- **Testing**: Monthly microbiological testing of water samples
- **Filtration**: Multi-stage filtration with UV sterilization
- **Hot Water**: Temperature maintained to prevent Legionella growth
- **Ice Machines**: Regular cleaning and testing
- **Dialysis Water**: Reverse osmosis with continuous monitoring
