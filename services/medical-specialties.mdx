## Cardiology

Our Department of Cardiology provides comprehensive cardiovascular care with advanced diagnostic and therapeutic facilities.

<Accordion title="Services Offered">
  - **Non-Invasive Cardiology**
    - ECG and Holter Monitoring
    - 2D Echo and Stress Echocardiography
    - Treadmill Test (TMT)
    - Cardiac CT and MRI
  
  - **Interventional Cardiology**
    - Coronary Angiography and Angioplasty
    - Pacemaker Implantation
    - Device Closure Procedures
    - Electrophysiology Studies
  
  - **Cardiac Surgery**
    - Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG)
    - Valve Replacement and Repair
    - Congenital Heart Disease Correction
    - Heart Failure Management
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Specialized Treatments">
  Our cardiology team specializes in:
  
  - Acute Myocardial Infarction (Heart Attack) management with primary angioplasty
  - Complex coronary interventions including bifurcation and chronic total occlusion procedures
  - Structural heart interventions (TAVI, MitraClip)
  - Advanced heart failure therapy including cardiac resynchronization therapy
  - Arrhythmia management and ablation procedures
</Accordion>

<Note>
**Emergency Cardiac Care**: Our Cath Lab operates 24/7 for emergency angioplasty procedures. For chest pain or cardiac emergencies, call our emergency line immediately at **+91-XXX-EMERGENCY**
</Note>

### Cardiac Rehabilitation Program

We offer comprehensive cardiac rehabilitation programs designed to help patients recover from cardiac events and procedures:

- Supervised exercise training
- Nutritional counseling and dietary planning
- Stress management and psychological support
- Medication optimization and adherence
- Regular monitoring and follow-up assessments

<Accordion title="Joint Replacement Services">

  - **Total Hip Replacement (THR)**
    - Primary and revision hip arthroplasty

<Accordion title="Trauma and Fracture Care">

<Accordion title="Sports Medicine">

<Accordion title="Spine Surgery">

## Neurology

Our Neurology Department offers comprehensive care for disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system.

<Accordion title="Neurological Services">
  - **Stroke Care**
    - 24/7 stroke unit with thrombolysis facility
    - Acute stroke protocols and rapid response team
    - Comprehensive stroke rehabilitation
    - Stroke prevention and risk factor management
  
  - **Epilepsy Management**
    - EEG and video-EEG monitoring
    - Antiepileptic drug optimization
    - Pre-surgical epilepsy evaluation
    - Vagal nerve stimulation
  
  - **Movement Disorders**
    - Parkinson's disease management
    - Essential tremor treatment
    - Dystonia and other movement disorders
    - Deep brain stimulation (DBS) therapy
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Headache and Pain Disorders">
  Specialized headache clinic offering:
  
  - Migraine diagnosis and management
  - Cluster headache treatment
  - Chronic daily headache evaluation
  - Botox therapy for chronic migraine
  - Occipital nerve blocks
  - Comprehensive pain management protocols
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Neuromuscular Disorders">
  - Myasthenia gravis management
  - Muscular dystrophy care
  - Peripheral neuropathy evaluation and treatment
  - Electromyography (EMG) and nerve conduction studies
  - Guillain-Barré syndrome treatment
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Neurosurgery Services">
  - Brain tumor surgery (craniotomy and minimally invasive)
  - Aneurysm clipping and endovascular coiling
  - Spinal cord tumor resection
  - Trigeminal neuralgia treatment
  - Hydrocephalus shunt procedures
  - Peripheral nerve surgery
</Accordion>

<Warning>
**Stroke Alert**: Time is critical in stroke care. If you experience sudden weakness, speech difficulty, or facial drooping, call emergency services immediately. Our stroke team is available 24/7.
</Warning>

## Gastroenterology

Our Gastroenterology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of digestive system disorders.

<Accordion title="Diagnostic Procedures">
  - **Endoscopy Services**
    - Upper GI endoscopy (gastroscopy)
    - Colonoscopy and flexible sigmoidoscopy
    - Capsule endoscopy
    - Endoscopic ultrasound (EUS)
    - ERCP (Endoscopic Retrograde Cholangiopancreatography)
  
  - **Liver Function Assessment**
    - FibroScan (liver elastography)
    - Liver biopsy
    - Hepatitis screening and monitoring
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Therapeutic Procedures">
  - Polypectomy and EMR (Endoscopic Mucosal Resection)
  - Variceal band ligation
  - Esophageal and colonic stenting
  - PEG tube placement
  - Stricture dilation
  - Foreign body removal
  - ERCP with stone extraction and biliary stenting
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Specialized Conditions">
  - **Liver Diseases**
    - Fatty liver disease management
    - Chronic hepatitis B and C treatment
    - Cirrhosis and its complications
    - Liver transplant evaluation
  
  - **Inflammatory Bowel Disease**
    - Crohn's disease management
    - Ulcerative colitis treatment
    - Biological therapy
  
  - **Pancreatic Disorders**
    - Acute and chronic pancreatitis
    - Pancreatic cancer evaluation
    - Pancreatic cyst management
  
  - **Functional Disorders**
    - Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS)
    - Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD)
    - Functional dyspepsia
</Accordion>

<Info>
**Endoscopy Preparation**: Proper preparation is essential for accurate endoscopic examinations. You will receive detailed instructions when scheduling your procedure. Fasting requirements typically range from 6-12 hours depending on the procedure type.
</Info>

## Appointment Booking

To schedule an appointment with any of our specialists:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Specialist">
    Select the appropriate department based on your medical needs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book Appointment">
    Call our appointment desk, use our online portal, or visit the hospital reception
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare for Visit">
    Bring previous medical records, test results, and current medications list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Consultation">
    Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled time for registration
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Specialist Consultation Hours**: Most specialists have fixed OPD timings. Please check the appointment schedule or call our helpline for specific consultant availability.
</Note>
